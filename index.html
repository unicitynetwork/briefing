Promote resilience features from opt-in to default-on
PRs #185, #184, and #181 flipped three feature flags from their conservative defaults to enabled-by-default: nostrPersistenceVerifier (#185), tombstoneGcWorker (#184), and orphanAutoRecovery (#181). Enabling these features by default means new SDK consumers automatically benefit from Nostr-backed persistence verification, garbage collection of tombstoned records, and automatic recovery of orphaned outbox entries without requiring explicit configuration. This reduces the risk of silent data inconsistencies in deployments that have not yet customized their feature set.
